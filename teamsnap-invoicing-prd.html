TeamSnap ONE Invoicing Feature PRD

Product Requirements Document

Author: Patrick Mader

Date: February 2026

Problem Statement

Core Problem: TeamSnap ONE migration is mandatory but blocked for organizations that rely on invoicing. This prevents reaching the 70%+ payment attach rate target.

Decision: We must decide how to allocate limited resources between removing a migration blocker (invoicing) and building a long-term growth lever (registration) to increase attach rate and payment volume.

Solution Overview

MVP (Phase 1)

  • One-off invoice creation
  • Line items & descriptions
  • Email delivery to families
  • Online payment link
  • Basic status tracking (draft/sent/paid)

Phase 2+

  • Recurring invoices
  • Installment plans
  • Auto-reminders
  • Late fee automation
  • Enhanced reporting

Why This Solution?

Unblocks Migration: Enables invoicing-dependent organizations to migrate from legacy to TeamSnap ONE

Captures Volume: Unlocks the ~20% of payment volume currently stranded on legacy due to invoicing gap

Enables Future Growth: Completing migration reduces operational surface area, positioning team to focus on payment attach rate improvement via registration features

Open Questions & Risks

Key Assumptions to Validate

  • HIGH Is invoicing truly the primary migration blocker, or are there other factors?
  • What % of organizations currently use third-party tools (PayPal, Venmo) to bridge this gap?
  • What is the actual revenue impact of the 20% stranded volume?

Risks

  • Scope creep in MVP could delay migration unblocking
  • Customer feedback may reveal invoicing needs are more complex than MVP covers
  • Phase 2 features may be needed sooner than expected

Questions for Stakeholders

  • What is the timeline expectation for migration completion?
  • How much engineering capacity is available for Phase 1 vs Phase 2?
  • Are there compliance/financial regulations to consider for invoicing?
